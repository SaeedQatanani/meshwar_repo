ARE YOU READY FOR AN ADVENTURE?

Our idea is to provide a platform for Palestinians to share and learn about different events in their area, or an area they are visiting, and to get new ideas about activities they can do anytime.

Our goal is to help you find your new favorite tradition with your friends, and to help others in your area appreciate all the hidden gems your city has to offer. It's the perfect antidote to your boredom!

We depend on user-generated content, so don't hesitate to add any events happening around you, and to share ideas for experiences other people can have in your favorite place.
